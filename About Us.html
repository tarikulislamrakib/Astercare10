Who We Are


Aster Care home is a licensed personal care home for seniors where comfort mingles with affordable budget. It is more than just a place to live. Our residents truly become part of our family. We always provide need base personalized care to our elderly citizen by treating them as if they were our own family. We know everyone’s needs are different, so our care plans are uniquely tailored to each individual.

We emphasis on combined team effort. The team comprises management, our staff and most importantly the family of our resident. We are dedicated to provide the highest quality senior care with dignity, compassion and peace of mind for their families. We’re not just any place to live; we’re the perfect place to call home!

Aster Care Mission


Our mission is to maximize the quality of life of seniors by providing personalized, high-quality care in a facility that promotes a safe, healthy, and caring environment.

Aster Care Vision


A safe and caring Continuum of Living for our resident.

Aster Care Values


To achieve our vision and mission, we focus on shared, deeply held values that govern our behaviour and guide our actions.

Safety & Care

We value the importance of privacy, safe, comfortable and home-like environment for our residents.

Compassion

We strive to be thoughtful and act with kindness and empathy towards all our residents.

Respect & Love

We believe every individual deserves to be treated with dignity, respect and and an acknowledgment of their value as individuals.
